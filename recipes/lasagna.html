Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a family favorite! It's quick to whip up on a weekday. It might not be the healthiest but it sure is a comfort food for the upcoming fall weather.

You'll just need your lasagna basics - noodles, ricotta, ground beef, tomato sauce, and lot's of cheese! It's easiest to use the parboiled noodles so it makes prep that much faster. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees and let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Precooked noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ricotta
  • Mozarella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Cook your noodles and set aside
  3. Brown beef and season with salt, pepper, and oregano
  4. Combine beef with tomato sauce and cook through
  5. In a large baking dish begin layering ingredients: Noodles, sauce mixture, ricotta, mozarella
  6. End with mozarella layer, then place dish in oven uncovered
  7. Cook for 20 minutes, or until cheese is browned and bubbly
  8. Enjoy!
